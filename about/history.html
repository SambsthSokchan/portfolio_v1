My Journey

A chronicle of growth, learning, and the relentless pursuit of engineering excellence. From my first line of code to building large-scale digital products.

2021 — 2023

The Foundation

Every great structure begins with a solid foundation. These years were dedicated to mastering the core technologies of the web—deep diving into HTML semantic structures, CSS architecture, and the intricacies of modern JavaScript.

I focused on understanding project lifecycles and the importance of professional workflows, setting the stage for more complex engineering challenges.

2024 — Present

Full Stack Evolution

Moving beyond the frontend, I expanded my expertise into server-side development, database architecture, and cloud infrastructure. This evolution allowed me to think of products as holistic experiences, not just as visual layers.

Today, I build scalable systems using Next.js, Node.js, and PostgreSQL, focusing on performance, security, and developer experience.
